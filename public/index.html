Header-Giver

How to:

1) Go to https://header-giver.herokuapp.com/api/whoami

2) Header-Giver responds with a JSON object containing your ip address, your preferred language, and your Operating System.

Examples:

Request: https://header-giver.herokuapp.com/api/whoami

Response: { ipaddress: "::1", language: "en-US", software: "Macintosh; Intel Mac OS X 10_10_5" }
